FAQ

Common questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

What's the difference between a chatbot and an AI receptionist?

A chatbot sits on your website and answers questions from visitors. An AI receptionist handles incoming messages (WhatsApp, DMs, inquiries) and can manage bookings and customer communication like a real front desk. A hybrid system does both.

Do I need a website to use this?

No. If you don't have a website, we can set up the AI receptionist via WhatsApp so you still get full automation. A website is only required for the chatbot feature.

How long does the setup take?

Most setups are completed within 24 to 48 hours. We ask you a few questions about your business, then we build and install everything. You don't need to do any technical work.

Can you customize it for my specific business?

Yes — every single system we build is 100% custom. The AI learns your services, your tone, your FAQs, your pricing, your hours, and exactly how you want to communicate with customers.

How much does it cost to run monthly?

Our plans start from $39/month. The AI runs on a small API usage cost which we keep as low as possible — some setups operate for as little as $20/month in usage costs, meaning our plans are priced fairly and profitably for everyone.

What happens if I need changes after launch?

Just message us. We're available every day and handle updates, new questions, hour changes, and any adjustments quickly. Updates are included in most plans.

How do I get started?

Simply contact us on WhatsApp, email, or Instagram. We'll start with a free consultation, understand your business, and recommend the best plan. No commitment needed to chat with us.
